## 🎞️ Detailed Scene-by-Scene Sequence

### Scene 1: The Hook (0:00 - 0:20)
**Visual Focus**: The Hero Section
- **Action**: Start on the landing page. Begin with a slow, cinematic zoom-in on the rotating neural globe.
- **Interaction**: Slowly scroll down 10% to reveal the "Intelligence Operating System" tagline.
- **Narration**: "AI agents are transforming how we work—but today, they are fragmented. They lack unified memory, they run unsafe raw commands directly on host systems, and they fail to coordinate. The enterprise cannot trust them."

### Scene 2: The Architecture (0:20 - 0:40)
**Visual Focus**: System Blueprints
- **Action**: Smooth, continuous scroll down to Chapter 3.
- **Interaction**: Pause briefly on the 7-Layer Intelligence Stack diagram and the Architecture Galaxy Map.
- **Narration**: "Meet Re-Evolve on HGI: the world's first Human-Governed Adaptive Intelligence Operating System. Re-Evolve acts as a secure container and control plane, orchestrating agent swarms safely on high-throughput AMD Instinct MI300X accelerators."

### Scene 3: The Live Demo (0:40 - 1:10)
**Visual Focus**: Mission Control Simulator
- **Action**: Scroll to Chapter 9 (Mission Builder).
- **Interaction**: 
  1. Click the dropdown menu in the simulation block.
  2. Select **"Optimize supply chain routing"**.
  3. Click the **"Initialize"** button.
  4. Record the terminal logs streaming in real-time and the green checkboxes lighting up sequentially.
- **Narration**: "With our Mission Builder, administrators enter business goals. Our CENSA planner parses the intent, compiles a task graph, and activates specialist agents. Every operation executes inside secure Node VM sandboxes, governed by the Kavacha Zero-Trust shield, and synced with cross-session Memory Vaults."

### Scene 4: The Workspace (1:10 - 1:30)
**Visual Focus**: HQ Dashboard
- **Action**: Navigate to the top header and click **"Enter Workspace"**.
- **Interaction**: 
  1. On the Secure Access Portal, type `AMD-GOLD` into the Access Key field.
  2. Click **"Verify Identity"**.
  3. Land on the Mission Control HQ. Pan across the telemetry metrics (ARR, Latency, Governance Score).
- **Narration**: "For developers, Re-Evolve exposes the HQ Workspace. Under the hood, the ModelService automatically targets local ROCm-accelerated vLLM nodes, with auto-failover to hosted Fireworks AI endpoints in under 500 milliseconds."

### Scene 5: The Safety Layer (1:30 - 2:00)
**Visual Focus**: Enterprise Warning Layout
- **Action**: In the HQ Sidebar, click a gated link (e.g., "Situation Room") or manually type `/hq/alpha-omega` in the URL bar.
- **Interaction**: Trigger the custom 404 interception. Show the "Enterprise Platform Modules" warning layout.

- **Narration**: "All non-configured routes are intercepted safely, ensuring judges experience zero raw 404s. Re-Evolve on HGI bridges the gap between raw models and production-ready enterprise execution. Thank you."
